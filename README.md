# 📦 inventory-risk-supply-chain-control-tower Dashboard

An interactive inventory-risk-supply-chain-control-tower dashboard built using **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to monitor supplier performance, inventory health, warehouse operations, and product demand.

This project follows a complete analytics workflow from data preparation to business intelligence reporting.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Supply chain teams require timely insights to monitor inventory, evaluate supplier performance, and understand demand patterns.

This project transforms raw operational data into an interactive Power BI dashboard that helps identify inventory risks, supplier delays, and product demand trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall supply chain performance.
- Identify products below reorder and safety stock levels.
- Evaluate supplier reliability and delivery performance.
- Analyze warehouse inventory distribution.
- Understand product demand across categories.
- Support inventory replenishment decisions.

# 📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard consists of **4 interactive pages**.

---

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of supply chain operations.

### KPIs

- Total Orders
- Total Demand
- Stock-Out Risk
- Delayed Shipments
- Total Warehouses
- Inventory Health Score

### Visuals

- Monthly Demand Trend
- Demand by Product Category
- Demand by Warehouse
- Delivery Performance
- Warehouse Delivery Performance

---

## 2️⃣ Supplier Performance

Evaluates supplier efficiency and delivery reliability.

### KPIs

- Total Suppliers
- Average Lead Time
- Delayed Shipments
- Supplier Health Score

### Visuals

- Supplier Performance Matrix
- Lead Time Analysis
- On-Time Delivery Analysis
- Supplier Performance Distribution

---

## 3️⃣ Inventory Risk Analysis

Monitors inventory availability and replenishment requirements.

### KPIs

- Current Stock
- Products Below Reorder Point
- Products Below Safety Stock
- Inventory Health Score

### Visuals

- Warehouse Inventory
- Inventory Status Distribution
- Current Stock vs Reorder Point
- Critical Products Table
- Inventory Alerts

---

## 4️⃣ Products & Demand Analysis

Analyzes product performance and demand patterns.

### KPIs

- Total Products
- Total Demand
- Average Order Quantity
- Top Selling Category

### Visuals

- Top 10 Products by Units Sold
- Monthly Demand Trend
- Demand by Product Category
- Order Statistics
- Business Insights
